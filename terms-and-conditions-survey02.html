Qualifying entrants:

  1. Entrants must satisfy the following conditions to enter the draw:
    1. live with the administrative boundary of Essex Partnership Board and
    2. within the boundaries of the following district or borough councils: Basildon Borough Council, Braintree District Council, Brentwood Borough Council, Castle Point Borough Council, Chelmsford City Council, Colchester Borough Council, Epping Forest District Council, Harlow District Council, Maldon District Council, Rochford District Council, Tendring District Council and Uttlesford District Council.

Prize Draw Entries:

  1. Entry in the draw is free.
  2. You may enter the competition by visiting the vision for Essex website and answering five questions about Essex.
  3. The Essex Partnership Board cannot accept responsibility for any error, omission, interruption, deletion, defect, delay in operation, communications line failure, theft, destruction, alteration of, or unauthorised access to, entries or entries lost or delayed whether or not arising during operation as a result of server functions, virus, bugs or other causes outside its control.

Prize:

  1. The Essex Partnership Board will arrange for the winner to be chosen at random from all entries received.
  2. The competition closing date is 31 March 2017 (5pm). The winner will be contacted using the telephone/mobile number supplied when entering the competition.
  3. The prize is £250 of ‘Love2Shop’ shopping vouchers. The prize is not negotiable and is non-transferable and no alternatives eg no cash will be offered.
  4. The winner will receive the prize stated and must include a day-time contact number when entering the competition.
  5. The Essex Partnership Board will make every reasonable effort to get the £250 shopping vouchers to the winner before the end of April 2017. Alternatively, the winner can collect the vouchers from County Hall, Chelmsford (identification will be required).
